**Distraction-Seeking Firefighter** (electric blue spiral)
- Strategy: Creates restlessness to avoid grief
- **Reminder:** If urge to get high/distract arises, pause. Ground first.

> "I feel very sad because my lifelong bestie seems to be cutting ties for someone he's met some months ago."

## The Specific Commitments to Extract

If he shows any openness, get CONCRETE commitments:

### What to Ask For:
1. **Weekly check-in:** Phone call or meaningful text exchange (not just memes)
2. **Monthly hangout:** Just the two of you, in person, minimum 2 hours
3. **Direct communication:** If he feels like pulling back, he TELLS you instead of disappearing
4. **Prioritization:** This friendship matters, not just convenient contact

### Red Flag Language (Means NO):
- "Soon"
- "Maybe"
- "I'll try"
- "We'll see how it goes"
- "I don't know"
- "Probably"

### Green Flag Language (Means YES):
- "Yes, I will"
- "Every Sunday at 7pm"
- "First Saturday of the month"
- "I commit to that"
- Specific days/times

## The Unblocking Decision

### DO NOT UNBLOCK TOMORROW

### Unblock IF (All Required):
- [ ] He takes real responsibility (acknowledges, doesn't just explain)
- [ ] He makes specific commitments with dates/times
- [ ] He shows genuine understanding of impact on YOU
- [ ] He follows through for at least 2 weeks

### Keep Block IF (Any Present):

- [ ] Defensive when you state your experience
- [ ] Makes it about his pain instead of hearing yours
- [ ] Blames girlfriend/circumstances instead of his choices
- [ ] Won't commit to specifics
